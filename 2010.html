IRISS Forum 2010

IRISS Forum 2010, our first annual Forum, was subtitled 'challenging times, creative solutions'. The focus was on the many ways in which solutions can be found by working in partnership, in order to deliver successful outcomes for the people who access Scotland’s social services.

Those who attended had the opportunity to hear from a number of speakers and exhibitors, and found out how IRISS can support the social services workforce to do things differently. The keynote speakers at the event were:

  • Adam Ingram MSP
  • Caroline Tomlinson
  • Janice Galloway
  • Andy Gibson

More information about the IRISS Forum 2010, including the full event schedule, can be found in the programme booklet for the day.
